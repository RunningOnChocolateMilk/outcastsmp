Anarchy Style

What is Anarchy?

Outcast SMP is an anarchy server with fair play rules to prevent exploits and ensure a fun experience for everyone. Outside of these rules and spawn protection, players have maximum freedom. This means:

⚠️ Important: Please read the Server Rules before playing. Breaking rules may result in warnings or bans.

Allowed Actions

  • Griefing: Destroy, steal, or modify any builds you find (except at spawn)
  • PvP: Attack and kill other players at any time (following combat rules)
  • Raiding: Break into bases and take whatever you want (except spawn)
  • Base Building: Build anywhere except spawn - hide well!
  • Freedom: Play how you want within the established rules

What This Means For You

As a player on an anarchy server:

  • Your builds are never safe (except spawn) - hide them well
  • Trust is rare and valuable - choose your alliances carefully
  • Expect to die frequently, especially near spawn
  • Items can be stolen at any time
  • Play freely while respecting the fair play rules

Survival Strategies

  • Build far from spawn (thousands of blocks away)
  • Hide your base underground or in unassuming locations
  • Never reveal your base coordinates
  • Use multiple stash locations
  • Form groups for protection (but watch out for betrayal)
  • Stay mobile - don't get attached to any location

The Spirit of Anarchy

Anarchy servers celebrate the chaos and freedom of Minecraft. Every action has consequences, and every player is both predator and prey. Embrace the chaos, and may the best outcast survive!
