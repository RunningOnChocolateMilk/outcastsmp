Outcast SMP uses a modified version of PowerTrims, a plugin that turns cosmetic armor trims into powerful abilities. This guide covers the custom configuration used on our server.

üìö Learn More: Check out the official PowerTrims website for the base plugin information, but remember our server uses custom values!

üéØ How It Works

To gain powerful abilities, you must wear a full set of armor (helmet, chestplate, leggings, and boots) that all have the same armor trim pattern. Mismatched pieces will not grant any effects.

Activating Abilities

  • Primary Abilities: Press Shift + F (Swap Item in Hand key) while sneaking to activate your armor's Primary Ability.
  • Passive Abilities: Always active as long as you're wearing the full set. No keybind needed.
  • Disrupt Abilities: Trigger automatically under specific conditions (like taking heavy damage). No keybind needed.

üåü Ultimate Abilities

To unlock an Ultimate Ability, you must perform a ritual using the /upgrade command. Place a full set of Silence-trimmed armor into the upgrade menu and initiate the ritual.

Silence Ultimate: Warden Transformation

The Silence trim's ultimate ability transforms you into a powerful Warden with enhanced abilities:

  • Rage System: Build rage by taking hits (1.0 per hit taken) or dealing hits (0.5 per hit dealt). Maximum rage: 150
  • Transformation Duration: 40 seconds
  • Warden Buffs: Strength II, Health Boost IV, Resistance I
  • Boom Ability (Shift+7): Explosive attack with 10 second cooldown. Damage: 12, Length: 20 blocks, AOE Radius: 3 blocks
  • Grasp Ability (Shift+8): Pulls enemies from 25 blocks away with 15 second cooldown
  • Leap Ability (Shift+9): Powerful leap with 25 second cooldown. Creates explosion on landing that breaks blocks and sets fire

üí° Tips & Strategies

  • Each trim has unique strengths - experiment to find what fits your playstyle
  • Full sets are required - mismatched pieces grant no abilities
  • Disrupt abilities activate automatically when you're low on health - use them strategically
  • Some trims work better for PvP, others for survival or mobility
  • Combine trim abilities with other server features for maximum effectiveness

üîÑ Commands

  • /powertrims or /pt - Base command
  • /pt resetcooldown - Resets all of your trim cooldowns
  • /trust <player> - Prevents your abilities from harming trusted players
  • /untrust <player> - Removes trust from a player
  • /trustlist - Shows your list of trusted players
  • /upgrade - Opens the ultimate ability upgrade menu
