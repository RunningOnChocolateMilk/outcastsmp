Survival Tips

Early Game Survival

Starting out on an anarchy server is brutal. Here are essential tips to survive:

1. Get Away from Spawn

  • Leave spawn immediately - it's the most dangerous area
  • Travel at least 5,000-10,000 blocks before settling
  • Don't build anything near spawn unless you want it destroyed
  • Use the nether to travel quickly (1 block in nether = 8 blocks overworld)

2. Hide Your Base

  • Build underground or inside natural terrain features
  • Never make obvious base entrances
  • Use lava or water to obscure entrances
  • Place blocks naturally - don't create patterns that stand out
  • Light up a wide area around your base to prevent mob spawns (but be aware torches can attract attention)

3. Multiple Stash Locations

  • Never keep all your items in one place
  • Create multiple hidden stashes spread out
  • Use ender chests if available (they're player-specific)
  • Bury chests deep underground in random locations
  • Mark locations with coordinates, not signs or obvious markers

Advanced Strategies

Nether Travel

  • Build hidden portals far apart
  • Nether highways can be fast but are dangerous
  • Never build important structures near nether spawn
  • Use nether roof if possible (though some servers disable this)

End Dimension

  • The End is often less traveled but still dangerous
  • Bases in the End can be more secure
  • Watch out for other players who know about End bases
  • End portals are public knowledge - secure them

PvP and Combat

  • Always be ready to fight or run
  • Keep healing items (gapples, potions) on your hotbar
  • Don't carry all your valuables when exploring
  • Learn to identify threats - suspicious players, modified terrain
  • Practice your PvP skills - you'll need them

Trust and Alliances

  • Trust is valuable and rare - choose carefully
  • Start with small collaborations before sharing base locations
  • Even trusted friends might betray you
  • Never share coordinates publicly or in-game chat
  • Use Discord for private communication (but still be cautious)

Resource Management

  • Don't build large, obvious farms
  • Spread out resource gathering to avoid patterns
  • Use renewable resources when possible
  • Store backups of essential items
  • Don't show off wealth - it makes you a target

Remember

Survival in anarchy is about being smart, hidden, and prepared. Every death is a learning experience. Adapt, overcome, and may the best outcast thrive!
